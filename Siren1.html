Title

The Siren And The Drowned Captain

Artist

Titan Lore Co

Medium

Digital Art

Description

A hauntingly beautiful dark fantasy illustration of a mermaid in the deep ocean depths. Her hair floats upward, transforming into bioluminescent jellyfish, as she gently caresses the stone-like bust of a lost sea captain. The scene is illuminated by the eerie blue glow of deep-sea creatures, creating a moody, gothic atmosphere. This piece is perfect for fans of nautical folklore, sirens, and dark ocean mythology.
