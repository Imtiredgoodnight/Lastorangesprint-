All About Sirens!

Beware they will kill you

Sirens are mythical creatures from Greek mythology.

Sirens are DANGEROUS do NOT go anywhere near them you will DIE!!! These deadly creatures will lure your ship towards rocks and they will drown you. Even if you can swim they will drag you under until the life fades out of you eyes. Their song is so soothing that it forces you to crash so beware for once their song hits you ears you're DEAD. They live on Sirenum scopuli which is off the coast of Italy, near Naples or Capri.
